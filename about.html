ABOUT ME

It always seems impossible until it's done, Nelson Mandela.

Hello and thank you for visiting my website. My name is Ishmael Sheriff and I am from West Africa, Sierra Leone. I am a student studying Computer Science in the United States. Though I am a computer science student, I learned to program website all by myself, I am a self-taught web designer and program. I am a full-time college student very determine and eager to make all A’s and B’s in college, but my love for web programming made me decided to teach myself web design and programming because I have always been curious about how websites are made. So, I decide to go the hard way and teach myself web designing and programming. I am a very curious and versatile person. Over the past few months, I have been building websites from scratch to finished and this my personal web portfolio will be my second website. I love learning by doing and learning by doing makes education lot more easier for me.
My aim in building any website is to make the final website reflect the business, company or organization it is representing. In addition, I also aim to make my website fully responsive and users friendly; I always make sure that my website can adjust and fit perfectly on any device the user decides to use to access the site. For an instant, this site that you are currently reading is fully responsive, meaning it can fit from desktop, laptops, tablets, and down to the smartphone with the smallest screen resolution.
Currently, I am a Front-End Web Developer, but I have also started learning Back-End programming by starting to develop web databases using MySQL, and PHP (A powerful scripting languages widely used by Facebook, WordPress and other networks). Like I said I am a curious learner; therefore, I am also eager to learn Android app development and in the nearest future I will be building an Android mobile app for smartphones by using JavaScript.
For other curious web developer, I completely built this website suing pure HTML, CSS3 some JQUERY plugins, and a bit of JAVASCRIPT. Though you are browsing through this site not, it is still under more development. Therefore, I will be updating and upgrading it continually as I put more work into what I love doing the most. Thank you for visiting once again and please o not forget to contact me if you want us to work on a project or if you want to leave more a message. In addition, please also like and share this page for me by using the tabs below. THANK YOU VERY MUCH and GOD BLESS YOU.
